Markerless Motion Capture Research

I focus on researching and applying Markerless Motion Capture technology. This revolutionary technology accurately captures human movement using computer vision and deep learning algorithms without requiring markers or sensors on the subject.

My current research directions include:

Multi-view Human Pose Estimation Real-time Motion Capture Deep Learning Algorithm Optimization 3D Human Reconstruction

By integrating advanced technologies such as OpenPose, MediaPipe, and AlphaPose, I am committed to improving the accuracy and real-time performance of motion capture, providing innovative solutions for fields including sports analysis, rehabilitation medicine, and virtual reality.
